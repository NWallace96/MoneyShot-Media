Why Twitter?

Advertising with Twitter is undoubtedly a great way of getting exposed to a defined audience. Arguably one of the best ways to create leads, due to specific targeting options. It’s pay-only-for-engagement policy means you know that your adverts aren’t just sitting there spending your money without benefiting your company.

Much like promoting your account, Twitter allows you to target specific aspects of your Tweet to access a defined audience. You can target by keywords; target by interests and gender; target by geography; by device; or by analysing your existing followers.

With the analytics, you can measure the success of your adverts and note what worked for you, and what didn’t, making it a great way to define your audience further.
